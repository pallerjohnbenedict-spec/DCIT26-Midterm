Questions that are frequently asked

What are the requirements needed?

Students must submit a copy of their Form 138, Certificate of Good Moral, and PSA Birth Certificate.

How long does it take to process and release documents?

Document processing typically takes 5–7 working days. You will receive a notification when your request is approved and when it's ready for release.

Do I need to pay for the documents I request?

Some documents may require a processing fee.

Where and how do I claim the document?

Once your request is approved, you can claim your document at the Registrar’s Office.

When will I receive a notification about my requested documents?

When your requested document is ready, you will receive an instant notification along with a release date.
